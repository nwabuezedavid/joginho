Grow with Sample Bank
  • With strength comes growth: Sample Bank started as a one-office bank and has grown into a regional financial company with over $25 billion in assets and nearly 230 locations in VA, WV, MD, OH, PA, NC, SC, and D.C.
  • A deep commitment to our communities: We are a community bank that actively looks for ways to strengthen the areas in which we live and work, and we encourage our employees to be involved locally.   • An appreciation for our people: We have over 3,000 employees who exemplify our core values of hard work, teamwork, integrity and caring. We truly value our employees and firmly believe that the service they provide to our customers and communities is our competitive advantage.

  • Diversity and inclusion: Our strength is our people, and we are committed to nurturing an inclusive culture that is reflective of the communities we serve; celebrates diversity of thought, backgrounds, and experience; promotes respect and a shared purpose; and aligns with our core values.

Financial Goals

Whether you’re saving for the future or striving for peace of mind today, Sample Bank offers a number of services to help you reach your financial goals. From 401(k) and employee stock purchasing plans to flex spending, financial planning, insurance and complimentary or discounted banking services, as a Sample Bank employee, you’re covered.

A healthy employee is a happy employee – from dental to vision, your medical concerns are covered. We even offer an employee wellness program to keep you feeling great.

Personal goals

Professional goals

With Sample Bank , you don’t just have a job – you’ve got a career. We offer numerous opportunities for you to advance, including internal training programs, external courses and conferences, and our Sample Bank Leadership Development Program.
